About Me

Date Mon 10 April 2017 Tags about

My name is Justin Naldzin. I'm a Senior Data Consultant living in Ponte Vedra, FL. I am very proud to be working at Caserta, a New York-based strategic consulting and innovation technology implementation firm.

Previously, I worked at Penn Medicine as a Data Engineer in the Data Analytics Center where I scienced the sh*t out of healthcare data.

I also worked for 10 years at Comcast as a Product Development Engineer - responsible for the operations and infrastructure of the Xfinity OnDemand platform.

I'm very passionate about technology and enjoy sharing knowledge to anyone with a similar curiosity. Most of my work is on GitHub with occasional blog posts at justinnaldzin.github.io. I'm not much of a socialite of media but you can reach me on:
